The concept

Safety training with a twist

Airline safety videos cracked the code on getting people to pay attention to information they think they already know. We're applying that same formula to the Young Guns warehouse floor.

Self-aware humour

Acknowledges the Rookie's scepticism before earning their attention — just like British Airways did when they made safety videos people actually watched.

All 9 SOPs in one hit

Every standard operating procedure covered in a single, cohesive narrative. No separate modules, no drop-off. One video, watched to the end.

Unmistakably Young Guns

Navy, orange, the warehouse floor, Rookies in hi-vis — and the Young Guns Way woven into every scene. This isn't generic safety content. It's yours.

Built to scale

One production. Deployed to every new Rookie in Australia, New Zealand, and Canada from Day One — for every cohort, forever.

The Young Guns Way

Values woven in, not bolted on

The three pillars of the Young Guns Way aren't mentioned once and forgotten — they're the backbone of the entire script.

High Care
Safety as an act of care — for your teammates, for your customers, and for yourself.
High Trust
Content that respects Rookies' intelligence earns their trust. The script never talks down.
High Achievement
Safe teams perform better. Every SOP is framed as the path to a stronger, more consistent shift.
